ABOUT US

Dedicated to delivering unforgettable rafting adventures with safety and excellence at the core.

History

Founded in 1998 along the Vaal River, Whitewater Rafting began as a small team of adventure enthusiasts eager to share the exhilaration of the rapids. Our growth has been guided by passion, safety, and a commitment to delivering unforgettable experiences.

Today, we host visitors from around the globe, offering expertly guided rafting trips that merge thrilling adventure with breathtaking scenery. Our reputation stands on a foundation of trust and respect for the waters that inspire every journey we lead.

Adventure awaits you.
